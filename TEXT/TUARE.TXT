^F06 Braedonnal Tuare

  As the elven commander of Tuornen's armies, this
  fighter would love nothing more than to attack Ala-
  mie and Rhuobhe.  However, he is held in check by
  other factors.  Influential but hot-headed, he has
  earned the nicknames "Hotspur" and "Firebrand".
