^F06Count Rogr Aglondier
^F02^W307Once a commoner, this count inherited his bloodline, royal name and the country of Ilien from the blooded count before him.  He is neither ambitious nor proud.  So, he rules the land fairly and wisely, and his loyal subjects love him for it.  Aglondier is the only known mage of the realm.
