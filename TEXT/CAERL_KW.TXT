^F02^W304Caerlinien, the capital city of Cariele, is home to the castle of the current ruler. The regent spends almost all his idle time and money overseeing the expansion of the royal castle. Royal vanity is reflected through his luxurious palace. The elaborate castle itself is guarded by soldiers and the regent's own powerful magics. Many of his treasures, including Barazad's Tools, are hidden away, only to be viewed by a few.
