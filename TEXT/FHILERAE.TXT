^F06 Prince Fhileraene

 Bearing a remarkable resemblance to Rhuobhe Man-
 slayer (a distant relative), this elf is an enigma to all
 around him.  He loves life, but can swing into moods
 of black rage.  He holds court with the most hate-
 filled of elves, and yet, he entertains traders from
 human lands.
