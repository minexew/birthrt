^F02^W304The Rose Palace is a granite castle sitting atop Thane's Hill. Somber and forbidding, it is surrounded by the twelve famous Yearlong Gardens. The reddish hue of the structure lends the keep its name. Many dark and almost unbelievable rumors tell of how it came to possess its crimson shade, but for certain it is a place that must be seen to be believed. Danica's Crystal of Scrying is hidden somewhere in this heavily guarded fortress.
