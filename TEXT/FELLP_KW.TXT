^F02^W304Kal-Saitharak is a castle of great power and evil. As the Gorgon's home castle, it has been fortified by the Gorgon over the millennia and now holds a number of his prized artifacts of power including the Chalice of the Dead. The castle sits brooding in the center of his domain and has broken many armies who sought to wrest control of the north from the Gorgon.
