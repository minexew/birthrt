^F06Guilder Kalien
^F02^W300This displaced Brechter took the reins of power from the unfortunate Endier clan.  He is ruthless, efficient, and very good at his trade.  Though not a gentle ruler, Kalien is canny enough to realize that an unhappy populace means an unhappy regent. In turn, he plies his subjects with festivities, balls, and holidays.
