This close friend of the Overthane is a very bright,
jocular fellow who is the leader of  Baruk-Azhik's de-
fenses.  He is in charge of an extensive spy network
and head of the powerful Royal Guild.  Watershold is
friendly to outsiders but devoted to the dwarf realm.
